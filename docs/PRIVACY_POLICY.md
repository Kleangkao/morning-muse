## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Shisa Sorasivisut ("we", "us") handles information when you use **Alice Daily: Alpha News**, including the **Alice Daily** web and mobile applications (the "Service").

## 2. Information we do not collect

**We do not request, collect, or store your wallet private keys, seed phrases, or recovery phrases.** Signing and approval flows are handled by your chosen wallet app; we do not receive your secret credentials.

## 3. News and content usage

The Service displays news articles, summaries, feeds, and related metadata from third-party sources. When you read content, your device may request data from our servers and/or third-party APIs (for example, to load articles or translations). We do not require an account to browse news in the web app.

## 4. Saved articles and local preferences

Saved articles, bookmarks, read-state, language preference, topic filters, and similar settings may be stored locally on your device (for example, in browser or app storage). This data is used to personalize your experience and is not sold. If you clear app or browser storage, local data may be removed.

## 5. Wallet connection and public addresses

If you use wallet features in the mobile app, you may connect a Solana wallet through Mobile Wallet Adapter or compatible wallet software. When connected, the Service may access your **public wallet address** and related public on-chain information (such as balances) to display wallet tools. Connection state may be stored locally on your device.

When you approve a transaction or message signature, the request is sent to your wallet app. We do not control your wallet's handling of that data.

## 6. Supabase and API services

The Service may use Supabase and other backend APIs to fetch news content, narratives, or related features. Requests to these services may include standard technical information such as IP address, device/browser type, and timestamps, as handled by those providers and our infrastructure. Supabase configuration uses public project URLs and publishable keys in client applications; sensitive server credentials are not embedded in the app.

## 7. Third-party services and external links

The Service may link to original publishers, Solana RPC endpoints, wallet apps, and other third parties. Their privacy practices apply when you interact with them. We are not responsible for third-party data handling.

## 8. Analytics and diagnostics

We may use basic technical logs or diagnostics to operate, secure, and improve the Service. We do not describe a separate advertising or cross-app tracking program in this policy. If that changes, this document will be updated.

## 9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated effective date will be posted in this document and, when available, on the Service website.
